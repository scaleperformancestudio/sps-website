# Creative Prompts voor Hero Animatie — VELORA

Gebruik de gegenereerde product-afbeeldingen (doos + stick) als visuele referentie bij het prompten. Alle 5 creatives moeten er uitzien alsof ze door een professioneel creative agency zijn gemaakt voor een echte Meta-campagne.

---

## Creative 1 — Hero Product Shot (DOOS + STICK)

Ultra high-end product photography of the VELORA Shilajit Honey Sticks container and stick. The deep plum purple cylindrical container with gold accents and honeycomb texture sits slightly left of center on a polished dark marble surface. Two honey stick sachets in matching plum purple with gold foil text lay artfully in front of the container — one sealed, one torn open with thick golden honey dripping slowly from the tip onto the marble, catching the light beautifully. Behind the products, a soft warm backlight creates a golden halo glow. Small dried vanilla pods and a tiny glass honey dipper are placed to the right as styling props. The background is a rich dark moody gradient from deep charcoal to warm amber. Every surface has realistic reflections and shadows. Shot with a 85mm lens at f/2.8, shallow depth of field with the product tack-sharp and background creamily blurred. Color grading is warm and luxurious — deep plums, rich golds, warm ambers. This should look like it belongs in a Vogue Beauty editorial. Absolutely no text overlays. Portrait orientation 4:5 ratio. Photorealistic, 8K quality.

## Creative 2 — Lifestyle Morning Ritual (DOOS + STICK)

A stunning lifestyle photograph of a woman in her early 30s with glowing dewy skin, sitting cross-legged on a plush cream linen sofa near a large floor-to-ceiling window. Warm golden morning sunlight streams across her face and body creating beautiful light and shadow patterns. She is wearing an oversized soft cream knit sweater. In her right hand she holds a plum purple honey stick sachet with gold text up to her mouth, about to squeeze the honey onto her tongue, eyes gently closed with a subtle satisfied smile. On the minimalist oak side table next to her sits the deep plum purple VELORA container with gold accents, alongside a ceramic cup of matcha latte with a perfect foam art. The interior is Scandinavian minimal — warm woods, cream textures, a single monstera leaf in a ceramic pot in the background. The whole image radiates warmth, self-care, and premium lifestyle. Soft golden color grading, warm tones throughout. Shot on a Sony A7IV with 50mm f/1.4, natural light only. The VELORA product should be clearly visible but integrated naturally into the scene, not forced. Absolutely no text overlays. Portrait orientation 4:5 ratio. Photorealistic, editorial quality.

## Creative 3 — Before/After Transformation (GRAFISCH + DOOS)

A polished split-screen social media advertisement divided vertically down the middle with a thin gold line separator. LEFT SIDE labeled "WEEK 1" in small clean white sans-serif caps at the top: a close-up portrait of a woman with visibly tired skin, slight under-eye circles, flat dull hair, neutral exhausted expression, looking slightly downward. The lighting is flat and cool-toned with a desaturated blue-gray color grade, conveying low energy. The background is a plain cool gray. RIGHT SIDE labeled "WEEK 8" in elegant gold sans-serif caps at the top: the exact same woman but now with radiant luminous skin, bright clear eyes, voluminous shiny hair, a confident warm smile looking directly at camera. The lighting is warm and golden with a rich warm color grade, she is literally glowing. The background shifts to a warm cream tone. CENTERED on the dividing gold line between both halves, overlapping both sides: the deep plum purple VELORA container with gold accents, shown at approximately 30% of the image height, with a soft drop shadow and a subtle golden glow behind it. At the very bottom of the image, centered: "VELORA — Fuel your glow" in clean gold serif typography on a thin dark plum banner. The overall design should look like a premium DTC brand advertisement you would see from Hims, Ritual, or AG1. Crisp, modern, trustworthy. Portrait orientation 4:5 ratio. Photorealistic faces with graphic design layout.

## Creative 4 — UGC Selfie Style (STICK + DOOS OP ACHTERGROND)

An incredibly authentic-looking UGC (user generated content) style selfie photo. A naturally beautiful woman in her late 20s with minimal makeup is taking a mirror selfie in a bright modern white-tiled bathroom. She has a genuine excited surprised expression — mouth slightly open in a happy "oh wow" look, eyebrows raised. In her right hand extended toward the mirror/camera she holds a plum purple honey stick sachet with gold foil text, showing it off proudly. Her left hand does a small thumbs up. She wears a casual white ribbed tank top with her hair in a loose messy bun. On the bathroom counter behind her (visible in the mirror reflection) sits the deep plum purple VELORA container among her other skincare products, integrating naturally as part of her daily routine. The lighting is natural bathroom daylight from a window — bright but not studio-perfect, exactly like a real phone selfie. The image has a very slight warm Instagram filter, minimal grain, and the framing is slightly imperfect (cut off a tiny bit at the top) to sell the authentic UGC feel. A trendy social media text overlay in a bold white sans-serif font with subtle shadow reads: "3 weeks in... my energy is INSANE 🍯⚡️" positioned at the top third of the image. This should look indistinguishable from a real customer's Instagram story. Portrait orientation 4:5 ratio. Phone camera quality, NOT studio photography.

## Creative 5 — Bold Hook Infographic (DOOS)

A scroll-stopping bold graphic advertisement designed for Meta/Instagram feed. Background is a rich deep plum purple with a very subtle honeycomb pattern texture overlay at low opacity. At the very top of the image, large bold statement in clean white sans-serif font (like Montserrat Bold), occupying about 25% of the image height: "Your energy shouldn't crash by 2pm." — the text is left-aligned with generous letter-spacing and line-height for maximum readability. Below the headline, three horizontal benefit rows evenly spaced, each consisting of: a circular gold icon on the left (row 1: a lightning bolt for "All-day energy", row 2: an elegant flower/lotus for "Hormonal balance", row 3: a crescent moon for "Deep restful sleep"), followed by the benefit text in clean white medium-weight font, and a small upward-pointing gold arrow on the far right indicating improvement. Each row has a very subtle thin gold line underneath as a separator. In the bottom third of the image, the deep plum purple VELORA container with gold accents is centered with a soft radial golden glow emanating from behind it, making it the visual anchor. Below the product: "VELORA" in spaced gold serif capitals, then "Shilajit Honey Sticks For Her" in smaller white text beneath. At the absolute bottom, a small clean CTA pill button shape with gold border reading "Shop Now →" in white text. The entire design should feel premium, bold, and editorial — like something from Ritual, Huel, or Athletic Greens. Typography must be crisp and perfectly aligned. Portrait orientation 4:5 ratio. Graphic design, not photography (except the product image which is photorealistic).
